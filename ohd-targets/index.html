What Is The OHD Drill?

Five steps. One group. No excuses.

1
Set up at 10 yards.
2
Fire 5 rounds slow fire, full focus on fundamentals.
3
Measure your group — all 5 rounds should touch.
4
Identify the flaw. Fix it. Repeat.
5
Progress to 15, then 25 yards as consistency improves.
